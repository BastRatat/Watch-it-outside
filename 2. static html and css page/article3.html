About us

Founded by Clara Dunn in 2015 in the cosy city of Boston, Watch it outside now employs 10 dynamic and creative employees. Our goals? Create outdoor films festivals that can appeal to audiences of all ages.

We already had teamed up with the mayor of NYC to hold a 4 days outdoor films festival in the heart of Downtown Manhattan : the Bryant Park. Following that success, we organized another event in Battery Park.

Today, we are excited to organize such an event in our lovely city. We expect 1500 persons to join our festival !
