Watch it outside Boston edition

Watch it Outside and the city of Boston have partnered up to offer you an extensive range of interesting films. We invite all of you to freely enjoy our selection of movies from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight in the wonderful Boston Public Garden.

From culture to history, Watch it outside offers you the best of documentary films projected on a giant screen in an extraordinary venue : the Boston Public Garden.

Outdoor movies are truly a unique summer experience for people of all ages, giving people one more reason to come together in a beautiful setting to enjoy our wonderful summers. We make sure to provide a variety of films, each appealing to a wide audience. We have performed hundreds of shows to date and the whole concept has been very well received, with people getting really excited to watch a movie under the stars with their blankets or lawn chairs. If you would like to hear from other groups that we have worked with, references are available.
